Become a volunteer

Join us and help others to reach their potentials!

We welcome interested high school students to join us as a volunteer to teach students STEM-related subjects or oversee the operation of the foundation. Our vision of the ideal volunteer at Action Potentials is thoughtful, versatile, and resourceful. All high school students are welcome to apply and become a part of this foundation.

The main tasks for a volunteer at Action Potentials consist of teaching other students, event planning, blog writing, and website management. Although you are not obligated to be involved in all aspects of this foundation, we highly encourage volunteers to do so as it increases your versatility overtime.

Volunteers at Action Potential do not have a long-term commitment requirment. For example, as a volunteer tutor, you will be linked with various students and are expected to schedule your own volunteer hours. However, if you are interested in participating in executive functions, a weekly commitment of around two hours will be expected.
