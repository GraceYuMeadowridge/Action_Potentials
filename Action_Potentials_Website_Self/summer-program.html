This Program

Immersive, interactive, online. Learning STEM-related subjects is made easy with Action Potentials

This summer, we welcome interested students between grade 5-9 to attend our inaugral Summer STEM Program. Our founders, Rayan Ramadan and Grace Yu, offer to teach students the basics of front-end and back-end website development, block coding, and computer-aided design. This program is free-of-cost and all interested students may attend.
