About Us

  • Home
  • About Us

Immersive, interactive, online. Learning STEM-related subjects is made easy with Action Potentials

During the initial lockdown of 2020, Rayan and Grace decided to start a STEM-related, free tutoring foundation name Action Potentials. Although the program was unable to attract many students, we decided to reintroduce and remodel the project into an all-in-one student-led foundation.

We believe that every student should have the opportunity to reach their potentials, as long as they commit to proactively learn. For those who are curious to discern, we are here to support your journey.

This summer, we are excited to re-launch this project with additional features such as STEM summer camps, blogs, additional learning resources, and an online project publisher. We believe that by supporting your learning, we will also acquire new knowledge, and we will develop our skills together.

An interesting, student-led foundation that advocates for teaching STEM subjects for all

  • - Interactive, online learning
  • - Free and accessible
  • - Taught by high school students
  • - Supported by our first-hand experience

Meet our Founders

Rayan Ramadan

A Determined, Principled Leader and a Curious, Proactive Learner
Rayan Ramadan is a dedicated, proactive student whose interests lie in molecular engineering and nuclear physics. She loves debating, challenging herself to growth, solving mathematical problems, lasagna, and pizza. She have a wide variety of interests, both in STEM and in arts. Curious and passionate, she enjoys research and public speaking, and she dreams to become a scientist in the future.

Grace Yu

A Committed and Passionate Engineer with Experience in CAD, Robotics, Data Science
Grace is a person who loves experiments and mathematics. Her passion lies in automotives, aerodynamics, and the mathematical models of different kinds of chaos. She used to compete in KF Senior class karting, thus she is especially interested in automotive racing, both as a driver and as an engineer. In the future, she desires to become an aerodynamicist for a factory team in GT Racing.
