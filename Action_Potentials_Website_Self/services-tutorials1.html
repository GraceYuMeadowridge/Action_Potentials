This Program

Immersive, interactive, online. Learning STEM-related subjects is made easy with Action Potentials

We offer 1-on-1 tutorials on STEM subjects both taught in school (Grade 10 or below) or extracurricular to support interested students. Whether you wish to learn ahead of the curriculum or improve your understanding of a a topic from previous years, contact us know and we will be glad to help you. For extracurricular subjects, if you have a few friends who are interested in a specific subject such as Coding or Computer-Aided Design, we also offer group lectures where you and your friends may all attend a personalized program to learn the subjects of your interest.

Both types of tutorial will be hosted on Zoom. Currently, we do not offer group lectures due to its schedule and content overlaps with our summer program. However, 1-on-1 tutorials are available. Take action and reach your potentials.
